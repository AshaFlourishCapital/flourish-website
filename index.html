The Problem

Climate finance is looking the wrong way.

The most vulnerable communities and ecosystems face accelerating physical impacts — yet the capital tools designed to help them are insufficient, extractive, and contracting. Proven solutions exist. They're simply not reaching the people who need them most.

01

Chronically Underfunded

Resilience and adaptation — the active strengthening of communities to withstand climate shocks — are structurally undervalued despite growing urgency and clear demand.

02

Broken Capital Tools

Venture capital and traditional debt are misaligned with the long timelines of climate adaptation. Traditional philanthropy is too siloed to drive systemic change.

03

So We Built It Ourselves

Climate resilience requires a different kind of capital — more flexible, more aligned with real-world conditions. We couldn't wait for the system to catch up.

Our Approach

Fit-for-purpose capital, built for the frontlines.

We recognize the deep symbiotic interdependence between environmental health, community wellbeing, and long-term prosperity. Our work spans three pillars.

Fit-for-Purpose Capital

The exact right financial tool for each problem — never one-size-fits-all. We match instrument to need across loans, equity, and grants.

Ecosystem of Action

A highly engaged community of founders, donors, and changemakers working in concert — convening the networks that make resilience last.

The Flourishing Index

A custom measurement framework tracking where planetary health and human wellbeing intersect. We measure what matters.
